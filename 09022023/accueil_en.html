CULTURAL HERITAGE


Spain under Muslim control


It was in 711 that the invasion of Spain by the muslims began. In less than 5 years, almost the entire Iberian Peninsula was under Muslim rule. As a result of this conquest, Granada became the capital of the kingdom of Granada, ruled by the Nasrid Muslim dynasty. nasride. 11 years after the beginning of the conquest, in 722, the reconquest of the country began: the Reconquista.
